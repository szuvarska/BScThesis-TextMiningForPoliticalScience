Title: Chinese top diplomat Wang Yi meets with delegation of foreign ministers from Saudi Arabia and other Islamic countries
Category 1: CHINA; Category 2: DIPLOMACY
Author: Global Times
Author title: Author details not found
Author description: Author details not found
Text: 
On Monday, Chinese Foreign Minister Wang Yi met with a delegation consisting of foreign ministers from Saudi Arabia and other Islamic countries in China.
During their visit, China will have in-depth exchanges with the delegation on de-escalating the current conflict between Israel and Palestine, protecting civilians, and justly resolving the Palestinian issue.
