Title: All Chinese citizens have safely left Gaza, said Chinese FM
Category 1: CHINA; Category 2: POLITICS
Author: Global Times
Author title: Author details not found
Author description: Author details not found
Text: 
Thanks to multiple efforts, all Chinese citizens have safely left Gaza, Chinese FM spokesperson Mao Ning said on Tue.
Mao said that since the escalation of the Palestine-Israel conflict, China has attached great importance to the safety of Chinese citizens in the conflict area.
Chinese diplomatic missions have been in regular contact with Chinese citizens in the Gaza Strip.
