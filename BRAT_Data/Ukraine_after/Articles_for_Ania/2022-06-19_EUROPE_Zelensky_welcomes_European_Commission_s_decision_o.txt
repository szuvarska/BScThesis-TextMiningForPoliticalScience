Title: Zelensky welcomes European Commission's decision on Ukraine's EU candidacy
Category 1: WORLD; Category 2: EUROPE
Author: Xinhua
Author title: Author details not found
Author description: Author details not found
Text: 
Ukrainian President Volodymyr Zelensky on Friday welcomed the positive assessment of the European Commission on granting Ukraine European Union (EU) candidate status.
"I commend the positive European Commission Conclusion on Ukraine's candidate status.
It's the first step on the EU membership path that'll certainly bring our victory closer," Zelensky tweeted.
